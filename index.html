QR-Swift Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 6, 2025

Overview

QR-Swift is a mobile application designed to let you quickly scan existing QR codes and create new QR codes directly on your device. The app operates entirely on-device and does not require an internet connection to deliver its core functionality.

Information Collection

QR-Swift does not collect, store, or transmit any personal or sensitive user data. Any QR content you scan or generate remains solely on your device.

Data Storage and Security

The app does not use remote servers or cloud storage. All generated QR codes and scanning history (if you choose to save them) stay local to your device's secure storage.

Internet and Network Usage

QR-Swift does not require an internet connection and does not establish network communications while you use the app. Your data never leaves your device through QR-Swift.

Advertising and Analytics

The app does not display ads and does not integrate analytics or third-party tracking SDKs. We do not monitor your activity in the app.

Permissions

QR-Swift requests camera access so you can scan QR codes. This permission is used strictly for the scanning feature and is never used to collect or transmit images or video data.

Children's Privacy

QR-Swift does not target children specifically, but it is safe for users of all ages. Because the app does not collect personal information, it complies with applicable child privacy protections.

Changes to This Policy

If the functionality of QR-Swift changes in a way that affects your privacy, this policy will be updated and a new revision date will be shown at the top of the page.

Contact

If you have any questions or feedback about this policy or the app, please contact the QR-Swift team at admin@dev2die.space.
